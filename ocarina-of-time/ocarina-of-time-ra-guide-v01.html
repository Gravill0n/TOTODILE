Chapter 1 — Inside the Great Deku Tree

Wake up in Kokiri Forest, acquire your Sword and Shield, then descend into the Great Deku Tree's interior to break Ganondorf's curse and earn the Spiritual Stone of the Forest.

1.1 · Boy Without a Fairy

  1. Watch the opening cutscene — Link's nightmare of a drawbridge, the white horse with the cowled woman and child, and the dark man on the black stallion.
  2. The Great Deku Tree summons Navi to find Link. After her overhead view of Kokiri Forest, she enters Link's house and wakes him up.

1.2 · Kokiri Forest

  1. Walk outside and meet Saria on the bridge below. Continue toward the Great Deku Tree's path — Mido blocks you, demanding a sword and shield.
  2. Head to the Forest Training Center (fenced area in the southwest of the village). Crawl through the small hole next to the sign.
  3. Wait for the giant boulder to pass, then quickly grab the two blue rupees and reach the chest holding the Kokiri Sword.
  4. Equip the Kokiri Sword and crawl back out.
  5. Collect 40 rupees around the village for the Deku Shield: blue rupees by the rolling boulder, the back-flip rock outside the crawl hole, chests inside Mido's House, rupees behind Mido's House, a rupee on the ramp by Saria's house, the water-jump rupees between Mido's House and the Shop, and a hidden five-rupee inside the Shop.
  6. Buy the Deku Shield from the Kokiri Shop (40 rupees, northeast building).
  7. Equip both Sword and Shield, then talk to Mido again — he reluctantly lets you pass.
  8. Walk the path to the Great Deku Tree. Slash the three Withered Deku Babas along the way and grab any Deku Sticks they drop.
  9. Speak to the Great Deku Tree. He explains the evil descending upon Hyrule and asks you to break his curse. Say yes and enter his mouth.

1.3 · Inside the Deku Tree

  1. Main Room: defeat Deku Babas to practice targeting + shield. Climb the wooden tower (ladder or vines).
  2. Open the chest at the top for the Dungeon Map. Skulltula #1 is on the climbable vines here.
  3. Cross to the door on the right and continue around. Use the Slingshot target switch later to open the door to the Compass.
  4. Drop down and find the Fairy Slingshot in a chest. Use it on the ladder switch and on Skullwalltulas blocking the climbable vines.
  5. Climb back up to the upper level, defeat the Big Skulltula and grab Skulltulas #2 and #3 in their respective rooms.
  6. Drop down onto the spider-web floor (jump from up high) to fall through to B1.
  7. Burn the web with Deku Sticks lit at the torch to drop down to B2 — the basement leading to the boss.
  8. Solve the basement room (push the block, burn webs, defeat Deku Scrubs) to reach the boss key door, then enter Queen Gohma's lair.

1.4 · Kokiri's Emerald

  1. Boss: Queen Gohma. As soon as the fight starts, stun her with a Slingshot pellet to the eye, jump-slash with a Deku Stick, then mash to hit her three times before she retreats.
  2. Collect the Heart Container.
  3. Step into the blue light to be returned to the Deku Tree, who gives you the Kokiri's Emerald — the Spiritual Stone of the Forest — before dying.
  4. Walk back through Kokiri Forest. Saria meets you at the bridge to say goodbye and gives you the Fairy Ocarina.

  1. From Castle Town, head east across Hyrule Field to Kakariko Village.
  2. In Kakariko Village, head to the Graveyard (back right of the village).

4.2 · Dodongo's Cavern

  1. Leave Goron City and head down to the Dodongo's Cavern entrance, blocked by a boulder.
  2. Pick up a Bomb Flower from the nearby ledge and throw it at the boulder to clear the entrance.
  3. Inside, navigate the cavern using Bomb Flowers to break walls, switches, and statue eyes. Light Deku Sticks at torches to reach the Dungeon Map and Compass chests.
  4. Acquire the Bomb Bag in the main room (kill the King Dodongo's child, Lizalfos pair).
  5. Solve the main room's eye-statue puzzle, light the giant Dodongo skull, then enter through its mouth to drop to the boss chamber.
  6. Boss: King Dodongo. Throw a Bomb Flower into his mouth when he winds up to breathe fire, jump-slash while he's stunned, repeat.
