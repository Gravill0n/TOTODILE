Chapter 1 — Peach's Castle & Hollijolli Village (Past)

The Shroobs invade the Mushroom Kingdom. You begin in the past as Baby Mario and Baby Luigi, lose the grown-up Bros to an unwinnable Shroob ambush in Hollijolli Village, then — by way of Baby Bowser's Star Cruiser — return to rescue them.

1.1 · Peach's Castle, First Visit

  1. Peach's Castle (Present) Watch the opening scene of the alien Shroobs invading the Mushroom Kingdom. You begin your adventure as Baby Mario and Baby Luigi in the castle (present time).
  2. Listen to young Toadsworth, then walk up the stairs to the next room. Continue walking up — you will pass three dead Toads further up. Baby Bowser then appears with the princess and burns up Toadsworth. When the Babies try to get the Baby Princess back, Baby Bowser attacks.
  3. Tutorial Battle: Baby Bowser: Health 8 · XP 0 · Very Easy. After your first jump, listen to Toadsworth: press X (or the other Brother's button) right as you land on Baby Bowser's head for triple damage. Dodge his flame attack by jumping over it right after he spits it. A few more hits finishes him.
  4. Watch the long cut scene; you move to present time, where everyone is grown (Princess Peach is an adult). After more talking, Mario is sent to fetch a Refreshroom for the fainted Toadsworth — you control him.
  5. Castle interior Head down to the next room and go to the bottom to find a Save Block (you do not need the restore block). Save, then go left to the next room.
  6. In this long room, get the coin from the '?' block to your left, then go up to two more '?' blocks: take the Mushroom from the left one and the coin from the right one. Go to the room to your upper left.
  7. Head straight down through three more rooms to the bottom of the third. Go through the door to your left (slightly diagonally up) and hit the '?' block in the middle of the room for the Refreshroom.
  8. Return to the middle room with the fallen Toadsworth and watch him get revived. Mario is then thrown into a battle against a green monster, the Junior Shrooboid.
  9. Tutorial Battle: Junior Shrooboid: Health 13 · XP 6 · Very Easy. Jump on him with A (you are Mario). He spins into you — jump just before he hits you to dodge, or jump right before he starts moving forward (not when he starts spinning) to counter. Keep attacking; the 6 XP levels you up to level 2.
  10. Watch a short cut scene; you can now control both Mario and Luigi. Go down to the next room and hit the heart block to your right to heal if you took damage. Save with the Save Block, then go down to the garden for the cut scene.

1.2 · Hollijolli Village (Past)

  1. Hollijolli Village (Past) The Brothers enter the time hole into Hollijolli Village. Listen to Stuffwell as it shows you the item stash — follow its instructions for Items, Equipment, and Bro Stats, and it brings you to the top screen. The time hole then closes, leaving Mario and Luigi alone. Walk right and hit the Save Block.
  2. Walk to the next part of the village; Stuffwell talks again. Jump up the steps to your right (both Bros can jump together). After two steps, walk up and left to the platform with the '?' block and hit it for 2 Mushrooms. Walk right to the next platform for 5 coins (rapidly hit the block), then take the 1-Up Mushroom from the third plateau.
  3. Continue right along the upper ledge; Stuffwell teaches jumping past gaps. Jump past three small gaps (both Bros together) into an area of Mario and Luigi blocks (each holds a single coin). Use Mario to hit the two 'M' blocks and Luigi for the trio of 'L' blocks, then walk up the stairs to your upper right to the grassy area.
  4. Walk left; Mario is hit by a falling Toad. After the scene, go up the wooden bridge to your left. You see Shrooblets on the Mushroom and Stuffwell explains them. Use Mario to jump on a Shrooblet to start the battle with initial damage. (Walking into them starts a normal battle; getting touched from behind can make you trip.)
  5. Enemy: Shrooblet: Health 7 · XP 2 · Very Easy. They come in pairs and attack by running into you (counter by jumping as they charge). Stuffwell shows how to control both Bros in battle (A for Mario, B for Luigi). Beat them in two turns. Possible item: Mushroom.
  6. Kill two more Shrooblets on the Mushroom, then continue up the wooden bridge to the next Mushroom. Kill another Shrooblet and walk up the pair of ladders to your right. As you go, Luigi is hit by a falling Shrooblet; Stuffwell explains taking damage and using items. Press Start when indicated, use a Mushroom on Luigi from the item stash, then press B a few times to exit.
  7. Continue up the wooden bridge to your left and kill the pair of Shrooblets. Walk up one last wooden bridge to a giant Mushroom with three Shrooblets. Kill them, then walk to the right of the large Mushroom across the short bridge into the city.
  8. Hollijolli city After the cut scene, walk to the upper right and enter the first house. Get the coin from the '?' block in the upper-left corner and three Mushrooms from the M block to your right. Exit, walk right to the house at the intersection, enter, and save with the Save Block.
  9. Exit and take the left path to the back of another house with an L block — use Luigi for ten coins. Back at the intersection, take the lower-right path to the house beneath the broken one. Use Luigi to rapidly hit the L block for 10-coin pieces, and get another 10-coin piece from the '!' block below it.
  10. Mayor: Return to the main intersection and follow the upper-right path to the Mayor's house. The Mayor is stuck in the chimney at the back of the house. Listen to him, then jump in the fireplace to pop him out of the chimney outside. After the scene, head back outside when he cries for help. You are thrown into a battle with adult Shroobs.
  11. Boss Battle: Three Shroobs: Health 18 each · XP 0 · Impossible. You cannot win this fight — a giant saucer kills both Bros after three turns. Before that, try to kill one Shroob. They shoot with a laser gun; dodge by making both Bros jump right after the Shroob fires (when the gun stops spinning). If the gun sparkles it aims for Mario, otherwise for Luigi. After three turns the saucer kills the Bros, and a cut scene moves to Baby Bowser's ship with the Baby Brothers.

1.3 · Baby Bowser's Star Cruiser & the Rescue

  1. Bowser's Star Cruiser You control the Babies on the bridge. A Koopa Guard shows you how to make the Babies jump (X for Baby Mario, Y for Baby Luigi). Jump across the crates, getting the Mushroom from the '?' block above the second lone crate. Continue across, grab a coin from another '?' block, and talk to the second Guard to learn jumping across gaps.
  2. Climb the crate stairs and jump across the gaps to the M and L blocks. Get 10 coins and a Mushroom from the M blocks, and a 1-Up Mushroom and a Mushroom Drop from the L ones. Jump off the bridge and go up the rest of the pathway to the second part of the bridge.
  3. Jump over crates as you walk left, getting the coin from the '?' on the second cluster, and use the crate stairs to pass the large cluster. Walk the last stretch, save with the Save Block, and go up the stairs to your left to meet Baby Bowser and Princess Peach. Watch the long cut scene (Baby Bowser willingly helps the Big Bros); you are sent to save them. Back as the Babies, walk to the front of the Mayor's house for a short cut scene and a battle against two Shroobs.

Chapter 2 — Bowser's Castle

The crashed ship drops you into Bowser's Castle. Work down through the lava halls — juggling the Big Bros and the Babies across switches, Split Blocks, and a pitch-dark Shine Sprite cave — to claim a Sparkling Shard, then return to present-day Peach's Castle for Professor E. Gadd's evaluation.

2.1 · Descent Through Bowser's Castle

  1. Bowser's Castle Save with the Save Block to your right and cross the path to your right into the part of the castle over a giant pool of lava. Get two Mushrooms from the '?' block and 8 Green Shells from the L one. Cross the first bridge and stomp the Goomba to start a battle.
  2. Enemy: Goomba: Health 10 · XP 3 · Very Easy. Goombas act like Shrooblets but are faster and a bit tougher. Jump on them to save your Green Shells, and counter by jumping right when they start running toward you. Possible item: Mushroom.
  3. Take the pathway down to fight another Goomba, then go right across another bridge and hit the M block for a 1-Up Mushroom. Back at the intersection, go right and up to a '?' block for 4 Green Shells, and kill the Goomba to your right. Continue along the path to an area with a very small bridge above you.
  4. Rather than jumping straight to the bridge, fight the Goombas to your right for experience: walk right to fight 4 Goombas (7 in battle). Grab a pair of Mushrooms and a blue coin from the '?' blocks in the middle. Walk back left and use the three steps at the upper-left corner to get on the bridge. Walk across carefully — do NOT jump sections or you will fall — jump the gap in the middle, and reach the platform at the end. Go through the door and jump to the low red bridge. A cut scene reunites the Big Bros with the Babies; you regain control of Mario and Luigi.
  5. Jump to the steps to your left; the Babies start to cry. Listen to Stuffwell, then run into them to put them on your back — you now control the Big and Baby Brothers together. Cross the stairs to your left and press the giant blue switch (with the Babies on your back) to open the giant door, then go through.
  6. Step in front of the tall ledge facing right and press X or Y to throw the Babies up. As the Babies, walk right and hit the regroup block (pipe picture) to bring Mario and Luigi next to you. Run the Babies into the Big Bros, then use all four to step on another blue switch, opening a door to a block-filled area. Throw the Babies off and send the Tykes through the small tunnel: jump the wooden bench to your left, rapidly hit the M block for 80 coins (10-coin pieces), and hit the L block for a 1-Up Mushroom. Exit and climb back on the Big Bros' backs.
  7. Walk right to the center of the room and hit all the blocks: the first two '?' blocks hold a coin and a Green Shell; the M and L blocks hold a Mushroom Drop and coins (rapidly hit the M); the last two '?' blocks hold two Mushrooms (upper) and coins (lower). Press the large blue switch ahead to open another door.
  8. In the ledge area, walk to the first ledge and throw the Babies on top. Hit the regroup block to your far left to bring the Big Bros up, climb on their backs, and save with the Save Block to the right of the ledge. Walk left to another ledge and throw the Babies onto the red platform above. Hit the purple '!' block directly to your left to lower the red platform halfway to the second ledge, then press A or B for the Big Bros. As Mario and Luigi, jump to the third ledge using the red platform as a boost, pick up the Tykes, and press the Blue Switch to open another door.
  9. Tutorial Battle: Boos and Goombas: Health 14 (Goombas) / 15 (Boos) · XP 3 / 7 · Easy. Stuffwell teaches piggyback moves against the Boo: jump to it, press X for Baby Mario then A as Mario lands; repeat with Y then B for Luigi. After the Boo, Kamek throws out a Goomba and Stuffwell teaches Bros Items with the Babies — Mario selects a Green Shell and Baby Mario rides it, so press X right when you hit for extra damage (best for only the first four kicks); Luigi then uses one (Y for extra). Finally a Goomba and Boo appear: use the shell on the Goomba but NOT the Boo (it floats and is immune to shells). The Boo vanishes and strikes from behind — counter by jumping a split second after it reappears. Finish with piggyback attacks; the Big Bros level up. Possible item: Mushroom (from Boo).
  10. After Kamek flees, climb the stairs, cross the wooden bridge, and hit the '?' block for a Green Shell. Jump on the Boo to your left for a battle with a new enemy, the Boom Guy.
  11. Enemy: Boom Guy: Health 18 · XP 6 · Moderately Easy. Looks like a Shy Guy with a cannon on its head. A Green Shell kills it easily, but practice dodging. Attack the Boo first; the Boom Guy approaches after the first turn. It has a delayed bullet and a fast bullet — jump when the cannon is about to fire (you auto-jump off a fast bullet); if a puff of smoke appears, quickly jump again for the slower bullet. Jump on his head to knock off the cannon; the Shy Guy survives but cannot act until he calls another (about 50% per turn). Tip: hopping on the Boo throws you into a fight with two Boom Guys and Boos — use Green Shells on the Boom Guys after countering the Boos. Possible item: Green Shell.
  12. Cross the rest of the bridge, kill two more Boos and Boom Guys for experience, and go to the lower-left corner to reach the pipe to the second part of the castle. Jump on the blue pipe with all four Brothers to go down; you pop up in the second part of Bowser's castle.
  13. Bowser's Castle (lower) Jump off the pipe and save with the Save Block to your left. Cross the path to the upper right. Hit the '?' block for a pair of Green Shells, go to the ledge on the right near the wall, throw the Babies to the ledge above, and send them through the door to a small cave. Hit the purple '!' block to grow the bridge near the Big Bros, bring the Babies back, and cross the bridge as a group of four. Press the large blue switch to open the door and enter the next area, then throw the Tykes to the upper-right ledge into another cave.
  14. In the cave, hit the purple '!' switch to form a bridge — but don't go back yet. Get the 1-Up Mushroom from the '?' block to your right, kill the two pairs of Goombas in the right section, and get two 10-coin pieces from the '?' block (20 coins). Walk near the lower step and press A for the Big Bros. Stuffwell explains Split Blocks: align Luigi under the solid block and Mario under the flashing one, make Luigi hit the solid (left) block, then quickly make Mario hit the flashing one when it turns solid — this creates a step in the Babies' cave. Make the Babies climb the staircase and line them under the Split Blocks: Baby Luigi hits the solid, Baby Mario the flashing one when solid. A platform to your far right descends. Exit through the left door and climb on the Big Bros' backs.
  15. Continue right (don't climb the platforms yet) and kill the ground enemies for experience — beware Boom Guy bullets, since back hits can trip you. Kill the pair of Boom Guys and hit the '?' block to your right for two coins. Go back a little, hop up the platforms to the upper ledge, dodge another Boom Guy's bullet, and kill the trio of Boom Guys. Walk to the wall above and throw the Babies to the ledge — do NOT enter the cave in front of you (it loops back). Jump up the few stairs to your left and hit another pair of Split Blocks to make another bridge. Return to the Big Bros, cross the bridge, and hit the large blue switch to open the door.
  16. In the final area, the blue pipe back to the castle's last part is blocked for now. Walk around the cage to the upper-right '?' block for 20 coins, then go to the opposite side to a giant Shine Sprite block (don't hit it yet) and throw the Babies to the ledge above into the pitch-dark cave. Make Mario hit the shine block first; a Shine Sprite pops out under the Babies. Use Baby Mario to activate it and quickly cross the bridge before the light fades — don't fall in spiked pits or hit the Big Shine block with the wrong Brother, or the Babies restart. Reach the third illuminated platform near the upper left, hit the Big Block with the correct Brother, and cross the bridge to your right to the next platform. Hit the big shine block and the small block again, then cross past the small platform to the large one at the bottom right (cross the rest of the bridge when it goes dark).
  17. Exit the cave with the Babies and drop into the cage. Hit the unique treasure-chest block to get a Sparkling Shard (it looks like a cone)! The cage gate opens for the Big Brothers. Regroup into a party of four, go through the door to the room with the blue pipe, and go down the pipe to the final part of the castle.

2.2 · Back to Peach's Castle

  1. Peach's Castle (Present) Exit the room and navigate back to the Professor at the back of the castle. Save and hit the Heart Block to heal if you need to. When you meet him, he explains that you have collected one Cobalt Shard and need 5 more.

Chapter 3 — Toadwood Forest & Vim Factory

A creepy forest of Toads turned to trees leads to the Shroobs' Vim Factory. Here you take down the L33T Hammer Bros, earn the Hammer Moves (so you can finally dig beans), clear the factory, and beat Swiggler for the second Cobalt Star shard — only for Baby Bowser to snatch both shards away.

3.1 · Toadwood Forest

  1. Toadwood Forest (Past) Step off the deck and save with the Save Block, then go right. Get 6 Bro Flowers from the upper-left corner and a pair of Mushrooms from the '?' block. The area has hollow tree trunks with goodies (or enemies) inside — throw the Babies into the first trunk for a Mushroom Drop (M block) and a 100-coin piece (use the Big Bros to grab it before it vanishes), then trampoline out.
  2. Throw the Babies into the second trunk to meet the Koopeleon, then a third trunk to your far left for a coin and a 100-coin piece. Walk up to the first Toad stuck in a tree; after his warning the Bros auto-dodge laser bullets. Send the Babies down the next trunk for a Super Mushroom and a 100-coin piece, then continue right.
  3. Enemy: Koopeleon: Health 20 · XP 6 · Moderately Easy. Attacks like a Goomba but turns invisible when it attacks — watch the small dust cloud and jump right when it moves forward to counter. Don't use Green Shells or Bro Flowers (only 1 damage). Possible item: Green Shell.
  4. Hit the L block for 6 more Bro Flowers and kill three Koopeleons. Throw the Babies into the upper-right trunk for 3 Green Shells and a 100-coin piece, then walk down until you drop onto a Boo Guy.
  5. Enemy: Boo Guy / Elasto-Piranha: Boo Guy (17 HP, 8 XP, Easy): splits in two and points at a Brother — jump both Bros, or counter by jumping after he finishes splitting. Elasto-Piranha (30 HP, 16 XP, Moderately Hard): spits a slow fireball from varying heights — watch the shadow and jump when it is a bit past halfway down. Do NOT use Bro Flowers on the Elasto-Piranha (only 1 damage).
  6. Work right and down through the trunks and Boo Guy / Koopeleon packs, grabbing the L-block 1-Up Mushrooms, until you reach the area with six small blocks around a larger one and many inaccessible trees. Drop the Babies into the accessible trunk in the bottom-left corner into a large underground tunnel.
  7. As the Babies, run the winding tunnel hitting all six Fire Blocks (the fire shoots up into the small surface blocks): hit the first at the first intersection, take the right turn and hit the second on the right side, then the next two along the path, fight an Elasto-Piranha and hit the fifth past the sharp turn, and hit the last after a bit more walking. Trampoline out and regroup.
  8. Now the six small blocks hold fire — hit them all so the fire gathers in the center block; after the sixth, hit the flashing red center block to burn down the trees blocking the Blue Pipe. Cross the new pathway and bridge, then drop down the Blue Pipe to the Vim Factory courtyard.

3.2 · Vim Factory Courtyard & the Hammer Bros

  1. Vim Factory courtyard Save with the Save Block, jump up the stairs, and talk to the Toad in the tree. Clear the Elasto-Piranha in the trunk and the 6 Koopeleons, then send the Babies down the left trunk for a 100-coin piece and a pair of Unarmed Jeans (M block — high power, very low defense; only equip on a high-HP Baby).
  2. Navigate the orange-platform spike pits (use the Babies on the arrow blocks to move the Big Bros' platform), collecting the Wallet Badge (L block), and clearing Elasto-Piranhas. In the next area, hit the center Split Block with a Babies-assisted jump, then hit the four Split Blocks in order — Baby Luigi, Baby Mario, Luigi, Mario — to power up the first claw at the entrance.
  3. Cross the second spike-pit area (beware the golden platform that pushes you off — Spin Jump the Big Bros over it), grab the Wild Trousers (L block) and Bro Flowers along the long final pit, then hit the second center Split Block sequence to power up the second claw. Save in the Blue Pipe area, then step on the blue pipe to enter the factory's second floor.
  4. Vim Factory Save again, step on the large blue switch, and watch the Hammer Brothers (speaking '1337') trap Mario and Luigi. You're thrown into your first true boss battle against both Hammer Bros.
    5. 🏆 Achievement here
    1337 H4XZ00R
    Beat both Hammer Bros in the Vim Factory's first true boss fight.

    1337 H4XZ00R

    10 pts Progression 75.34%

    "Defeat L33T HAMM3R BROZ. inside Vim Factory in Toadwood Forest."

    How to obtain

    1. Story mini-boss. After the Hammer Brothers trap Mario and Luigi (speaking '1337'), you fight both of them — 100 HP each, 80 XP.
